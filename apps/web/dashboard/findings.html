Finding contract

Triage fields.

Required fields
Title, category, severity, confidence, description, business impact, evidence, reproduction steps, fix recommendation, and status.
Normalized statuses
open, fixed, accepted_risk, false_positive, needs_review.
Safety boundary
Evidence summaries must be short, redacted, and appropriate for report display. Raw headers, cookies, API keys, private documents, large transcripts, and customer data are not stored here.
