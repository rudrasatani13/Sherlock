Security and cost controls

Why limits exist.

  • Protect against cost abuse. Unbounded prompt execution, tool calls, and retries are forbidden. Every scan mode has a max test count and timeout.
  • Protect targets. Every scan requires a verified target. Unverified or unauthorized targets cannot be scanned.
  • Prevent unbounded consumption. Concurrency, response size, and monthly scan counts are capped per scan type and plan tier.
  • Keep reports manageable. Report levels are matched to scan depth. Deep scans produce detailed reports; quick scans produce summaries.
  • Rate limit and deny-of-wallet controls. Future phases will add rate limits, spend controls, and abuse detection.
